Shantiviharcharaja

History

Shanti Vihar Ganpati Mandal, started in 2000, has been a symbol of devotion and unity in Mira Road for over two decades. Every year, we celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi with cultural events, aartis, and eco-friendly themes. What began as a small gathering has become a grand celebration of faith and togetherness.
